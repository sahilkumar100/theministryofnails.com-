About Us

The director, Princy Jaiswal is an MBA - Marketing and has been working for over a decade in Delhi NCR. She has always had a creative bent of mindset which was in fact an integral part of her professional career. Coming from a boarding school background started taking risks very early in life. She has always been a type of women who steps up to the challenge even when she doesn’t know what the result would be. All these has led to the incorporation of this entrepreneurship venture. This is also a step towards her visualization and knowledge of art and getting it to lower tier towns. The Ministry of Nails is incorporated with a vision to bring luxury creativity to the doorstep of the women in lower TIER towns/ cities. A one-stop haven for a luxurious nail affair. Also, with a bent of mind to work towards upliftment in lifestyle of women in these towns, MofN is one of a step towards it. Whatever you are feeling, let your nails be the extension of your mood - Princy Jaiswal
